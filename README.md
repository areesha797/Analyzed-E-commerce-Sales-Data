# Analyzed-E-commerce-Sales-Data
This interactive dashboard provides a 360-degree view of an Ecommerce Business's performance. It analyzes sales trends, profitability, and customer purchasing patterns across different regions and categories. The goal is to identify high-performing segments and optimize payment and logistics strategies.

# Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
The dashboard tracks four primary business health metrics:
Total Sales (Sum of Amount): 438K
Total Quantity Sold: 5,615 units
Total Profit: 37K
Average Order Value: 121K

# Key Business Insights
Geographic Performance: Maharashtra leads in sales volume, followed by Madhya Pradesh and Uttar Pradesh.
Category Breakdown: Clothing is the dominant category, accounting for 63% of the total quantity sold, followed by Electronics (21%) and Furniture (17%).
Monthly Profit Trends: The business experienced peak profitability in January and November, while facing losses in months like May and July, indicating seasonal fluctuations.
Payment Preferences: Cash on Delivery (COD) is the most popular payment method at 44%, followed by UPI (21%), highlighting consumer trust in offline payments.
Product Profitability: Printers and Bookcases are the top-performing sub-categories in terms of profit contribution.
Top Customers: High-value customers like Harivansh and Madhav are identified, which is crucial for loyalty program targeting.

 # Tech Stack & Features
Visualizations: Donut charts for categorical data, Bar charts for geographical and customer ranking, and Line/Area charts for time-series profit analysis.
Interactivity: Integrated Quarterly Slicers (Q1-Q4) and State Filters to allow users to drill down into specific timeframes and regions.
